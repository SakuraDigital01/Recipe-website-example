This is how to make Ramen

ingredients

  • Tonkotsu broth
  • Noodles
  • Eggs
  • Seaweed wrap
  • Spring onion

Method

  1. Cook up the broth
  2. Soft boil the eggs and put in the ice water bath
  3. Put the noodles in boiling water
  4. Assemble the broth, eggs and noodles
  5. Serve with seaweed and spring onions to garnish
